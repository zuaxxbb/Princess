Chapter One

About Her

♛
A princess, in the truest sense…

Charming, elegant, endlessly her own.

She moves through the world the way sunlight moves through gold — quietly, gracefully, and impossible to ignore. There is a softness in the way she speaks, and a stillness in the way she listens, that makes ordinary rooms feel a little more like ballrooms.

Beneath that grace lives a quiet fire. She is fiercely dedicated to the things and people she loves, and there is a beautiful stubbornness in the way she chases what matters to her. Soft, yet strong. Delicate, yet unshakable.

She is the rarest kind of girl — the kind who is both the calm and the spark, the poem and the storm.

Chapter Two

Her Favourite Drink

✦
A tiny, fizzy little ritual…

One cold Coke, and the whole world softens.

There is something wonderfully unserious about the way she treats herself to a Coke. That first crack of the can, the hiss, the fizz — it is her tiny standing ovation, her own little celebration tucked into an ordinary day.

You can almost see her mood shift with the first sip. A quiet smile, a little sigh, shoulders dropping. It is the smallest luxury in her royal treasury, and somehow, it is her favourite.

Not champagne. Not tea in bone china. Just Coke — and the pure, unfiltered joy of a girl who knows exactly what makes her happy.

Chapter Three

Her Highness

♛
A secret only a few get to see…

Tough on the outside, tiny princess on the inside.

To the world, she is composed. Sharp. Unbothered. She walks in with that quiet, unreadable calm that makes people sit up a little straighter.

But underneath — oh, underneath — she is the softest little thing. All giggles and shy smiles, tiny excited noises, dramatic pouts and cheeks that go pink over the smallest compliments. There is a baby princess hiding in there, and she only comes out for the people she trusts.

Her laugh is my favourite sound in the world. It escapes when she least expects it — a small, bright thing that turns her from queen to little kid in a single breath. And every time I hear it, I fall in love again.

Chapter Four

Her Hobby

❦
Where she quietly disappears…

A good book, a soft light, and she is home.

Give her a book and watch the whole world slow down. Her shoulders settle, her face softens, and somewhere between the first and second page, she is gone — tucked inside a story that only she can hear.

Reading is her little escape, her quietest happiness. It is where her imagination lives out loud, where her thoughtful, dreamy side finally has room to breathe. She loves the hush of a good chapter, the weight of a well-worn cover, the smell of pages that have been loved.

Watching her read is like watching someone come home. Peaceful. Content. Utterly, unmistakably herself.

Chapter Five

Her Favourite Flowers

✿
A bouquet of soft, quiet things…

She loves the flowers that look like poems.

White lilies. White tulips. Moonlight flowers. Blue hydrangeas. Every one of them delicate, elegant, and just a little bit dreamy — much like the girl who loves them.

Put her in a room full of soft petals and something inside her lights up. Her whole face changes. It is the same look she gets when she finds a good book or hears a song she loves — pure, honest, unfiltered happiness.
